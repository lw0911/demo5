学校简介

作者：爱为书院 发布日期：2020-02-15 15:34:21

爱为书院(A+W ACADEMT)是由华为控股投资公司出资，由清华附中负责学校运营管理的一所学校。

A+W ACADEMY IS A SCHOOL FUNDED BY HUAWEI AND MANAGED BY TSINGHUA UNIVERSITY HIGH SCHOOL

学校采用清华附中国际课程，以中西合璧的教育理念，博采中国教育和国际教育之所长,保护中国文化中的精髓部分，提供扎根中国、清华特色的国际教育。

The curriculum is called Tsinghua University High School International Curriculum, which integrates Chinese and Western education philosophy,local and international education essence, with heritages of traditional Chinese ethos and Tsinghua characteristics.

我们的招生对象为学前班至12年级的学生，学段划分为：K学前班，1-5小学，6-8初中，9-12高中。学前至小学阶段采用沉浸式中英双语教学模式，初高中则逐步过渡到以英文教学为主，为学生进入国际一流大学做好语言上的准备。

We provide education to students from kindergarten to grade 12. The four schools are Kindergarten, Primary School from grades 1 to 5, JuniorHigh School from grades 6 to 8, and Senior High School from grades 9 to 12. From kindergarten to primary school, students immerse in a Chinese-English bilingual environment. From junior high to senior high school, students will gradually transit to English only instructions and be prepared for future top international university education.

清华附中将学校的教育使命定位为“培养无限可能的人才，创造无限可能的世界”。我们努力提供多彩教育平台，整合最优教育资源，采用丰富教学模式，把提高学生的人文素养渗透到文化课学习以及体育教育、科技教育和艺术教育之中。在具备丰富人文积累、创新思维和知识储备的基础上培养独立思想、自由精神和责任意识。

The mission of Tsinghua University High School is Cultivating the talent with infinite possibilities, and Creating the world of infinite possibilities. We strive to build an education platform, integrate the best education resources and teaching practices, to improve the students' humanities spirit through the academic lessons, physical education, science and technology education and art education. On the basis of cultural accumulation, innovation thinking and knowledge reserve, we cultivate the thought of independence, the spirit of freedom and the consciousness of responsibility.
